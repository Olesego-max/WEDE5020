History of Pick n Pay

Pick n Pay was founded by Ackerman Raymond in Cape Town in 1967, and it did not start at a large scale as today in South Africa. It started as a small business supermarket business and grew into one of South Africa’s biggest retail chains across many African countries. This company mostly provides groceries at affordable prices for price conscience customers.
